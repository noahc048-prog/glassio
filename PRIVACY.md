# Privacy Policy for Glassio

Effective Date: December 14, 2025

Glassio is a macOS inventory management application.

## Data Collection
Glassio does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.
All information entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s Mac.

## Third-Party Services
Glassio does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## User Accounts
Glassio does not require user registration or login.

## Data Usage
All data created within the app remains under the user’s control and is not transmitted off the device.

## Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated if app functionality changes.

## Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
support@glassio.app
